Our Services

Shop

In our shop section,

  • We bring you choices of art of various categories from various artists around the world.
  • We deliver your items to you where ever you are globally.
  • We make it easier for artists to sell art.
  • We give a very considerable discount according to our "customer status" policy.




Connect

In our connect section,

  • We bring a means for artists and their audiences to talk art.
  • We bring artists around the world a means to share ideas, inspirations, thoughts and other mind resources together
  • Artists are given resources to help them improve. Promo and awards are also give to our most prominent artists to improve their recognition globally.
